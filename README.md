# FinCharter
A self-contained, privacy-first financial tracking and operating system desktop application.  It allows you to model your current finances across acounts and simulate future financial decisions with precise W-4 paycheck modeling.  Entirely offline, with zero AI, and zero cloud dependencies.


# The FinCharter Privacy Manifesto

Most modern financial applications treat your personal data as a commodity. They force your data onto cloud servers, use your transaction history to train AI models, and introduce data-breach vulnerabilities to your most sensitive information.

FinCharter was built to change that. It is a financial tracking and operating system designed with an uncompromising commitment to security, privacy, and absolute user control.

We operate under five unshakeable pillars:

### 1. Zero External API Calls
Once downloaded, FinCharter is entirely self-contained. The application does not talk to any external servers, third-party aggregators, or analytics trackers. Your financial footprint stops at your physical machine.

### 2. No Artificial Intelligence
FinCharter does not use AI. Your budgeting, paycheck modeling, and future financial simulations are driven strictly by precise math, deterministic W-4 algorithms, and your own template overlays. Your data is never used to train a machine learning model.

### 3. Air-Gapped Updates
FinCharter will never ping a remote server to "check for updates." We believe in-app update systems break the air-gap promise. Updates are handled entirely externally through secure, OS-level app stores (like the Microsoft Store), keeping the application completely silent on your network.

### 4. Encrypted, Local Storage
Your data lives exactly where you put it. FinCharter features one-button exports that create encrypted files saved locally on your device.

We operate no cloud infrastructure for storing customer financial records. That means if FinCharter itself were compromised, attackers would not discover centralized databases containing your budgeting history, transactions, or financial plans — because we do not collect or store that information remotely.  Your financial records remain accessible even if FinCharter disappears. 
Likewise, we cannot provide customer financial records to third parties or authorities from servers we do not possess. The data remains under your control, on your hardware, in your storage locations.
### 5. Source-Available Trust
Trust should be verified, not assumed. The entire source code for FinCharter is viewable on GitHub under the Business Source License (BSL). Anyone with the technical know-how can inspect our codebase to verify our zero-network claims, or even compile the application themselves for free.

### 6. You are Sovereign Over Your Data
FinCharter was built on a simple principle: your financial data belongs to you alone.

We do not collect your financial records, store them on remote servers, sell them to advertisers, or use them to train machine learning systems. FinCharter exists solely to help you organize, model, and understand your finances on your own terms.

Your records remain under your control, on your hardware, in formats you can export, inspect, back up, and keep indefinitely.
